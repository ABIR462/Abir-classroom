Enhancing the Look of a Presentation

Chapter Suggestions for Professional PowerPoint Design

1. Using Templates

Templates provide a quick way to create professional presentations with predefined layouts, fonts, and colors.

  • Choose from built-in templates or download new ones.
  • Apply to maintain a consistent look.
  • Customize text and images as per your needs.

2. Applying Themes

Themes help ensure visual consistency across all slides.

  • Access themes from the Design tab.
  • Apply to one slide or the whole presentation.
  • Keep your branding colors in mind while choosing themes.

3. Changing Slide Backgrounds

Backgrounds add personality to your presentation but should not distract the audience.

  • Use solid colors, gradients, or images as backgrounds.
  • Keep text readability in mind.
  • Avoid cluttered or very bright backgrounds.

4. Using Color Schemes

Color schemes enhance the design and set the tone of your presentation.

  • Follow brand or topic-appropriate palettes.

5. Customizing Fonts

Fonts impact readability and audience engagement.

  • Use professional, clean fonts (like Calibri, Arial).
  • Avoid decorative fonts for body text.
  • Keep headings bold and consistent.

6. Adding Design Elements

Shapes, icons, and SmartArt improve visual appeal.
